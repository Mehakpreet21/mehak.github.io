Post-Trauma

The trauma'a over, the thunder's gone,
It's high time sweetie, you gotta move on.
It's time you grow up, and realise who's worth your time
Because there's not many people who'd really give a dime.
They didn't appreciate you, didn't see your worth,
But darling, a diamond's a diamond, even if it came from beneath the earth.
So who cares if they didnt come back apologising?
Because trust me, in no time, they will be realising.
They'll miss having you around, sweetheart,
And they'll know you were worth it from the start.
But be glad it's over, you know it wasn't meant to be,
Them not giving a damn, but you still caring, like, seriously?
It's okay to still care, but let them know you're your own prime
Don't let them come back, because if they wanted to, they'd done it right the first time.
Be a little selfish, it's the only way you'll make it till the end
Because that's the problem with putting others first, you've taught them you come second.
