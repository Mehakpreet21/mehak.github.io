The Fall

by Mehakpreet Kaur

The Fall

People say be proud of who you are, but what if you're a mess?
What they dont know is that a smile doesn't always mean happiness.

All this time, I felt it was real
Daydreaming about you, almost every meal.

Shattering me in pieces was somewhat cruel of you
But worse of me, for letting you through.

I could have been the best thing in your life,
But that was until I knew, you had the guts to hold the knife.

Hah, how funny it must have been to see me suffer
Making me wait for it to resume, while you stopped it's buffer

And after all this while, i'm still so in love with you,
Soothing myself with the knowledge that at least my part was true.

Because people don't fall in and out of love so easily
It's when the "infatuation" ends, accompanying the glee

You made me this, at least now i'm stronger.
I can say I survived a heartbreak, after a while longer.

I'll come out better, and stronger than ever
I'll prove my belief in the long lost forever

There are two kinds of revenges, i choose the bitter one
I'll be happier without you, you haven't yet won.

They say "hurt an artist, and you'll see masterpieces of what you've done"
But pardon me sir, I've merely just begun.
